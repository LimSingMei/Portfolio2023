DESCRIPTION


This idea comes from my everyday diet, which is the daily food I eat. As the food contains lots of varieties and I also quite familiar with them, it is easier for me to come up with the designs. Thus, I think food is suitable to create a set of fonts with different appearances.



Since I need to suit the idea with every alphabet, I not only need to know about how to draw the food, I also need to consider about the alignment and shape of the food without extremely changing their original appearance.



I enjoy eating and trying new food so that I choose it for my font design idea. If this font can be used in the future, I hope it to be used in making posters or advertisements related to food. It will be cute and interesting!
