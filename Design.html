DESCRIPTION


This poster is created according to the 14st goal of the UN SDG 17 Goals which is the “Life below water”. The goal is mainly related to the ocean so I decide to design a set of sailor suits for the BB Bear to emphasize the theme.



Beside that, to link the theme with the beverage, I take the sea creatures into the consideration. Inside the beverage cup, I draw a whale emerging from the surface of the ocean and some of the small fish swimming in the ocean. There are a variety of marine plants such as seaweeds and colourful corals in the cup so as to highlight the beauty of the ocean.



The position of the BB Bear also displays the curiosity and the favor towards the ocean. Thus, the poster bring out the meaning of protecting and preserving the nature so as to avoid from critical pollution.
